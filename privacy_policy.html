Short version: Did You Know? does not collect, store, or share any personal data whatsoever. The app works fully offline and requires no account, login, or internet connection. Your privacy is completely protected.

This Privacy Policy describes how Did You Know? — Fun Facts ("the App", "we", "our") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all users of the App.

By using the App, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect absolutely no personal information.

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Name, email address, or contact information

3. Internet & Permissions

Did You Know? works 100% offline. The App does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests.

The App requests only the following Android permission:

4. Third-Party Services

Did You Know? does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking libraries. There are no third parties involved in the operation of this App.

  • No Google Analytics or Firebase

  • No advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No social media integrations

5. Data Storage & Security

Since no user data is collected, there is no user data stored on any server. All content within the App (fun facts) is bundled locally within the App itself and never leaves your device.

Your device's own security (screen lock, encryption) protects your local data as it would any other app on your phone.

Parents can be confident that their children's privacy is fully protected when using this App.
